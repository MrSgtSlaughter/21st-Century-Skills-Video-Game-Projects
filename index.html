21st Century Skills Slaughterhouse Videogame Projects

Showcasing 17 Student Technical Designs

Project Requirements & Skills

Students were tasked with designing a playable experience that demonstrates:

  • Critical Thinking: Logical game flow and bug testing.
  • Creativity: Original asset design and storytelling.
  • Collaboration: Peer review and technical feedback.
  • Information Literacy: Managing digital assets and code blocks.
  • Persistence: Solving complex "Slaughterhouse" logic puzzles.
